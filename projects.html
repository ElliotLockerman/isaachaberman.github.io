I analyzed the partisan dataset, which contains data on a survey done in a former Soviet bloc country in 1998 and 2003. The researchers theorized that demographics categorized citizens political views which in turn categorized their views on history and human rights. To test the theory of the researchers, I clustered the opinions of the citizens using mixture models, tested for predictability and independence between the clusters before concluding.
